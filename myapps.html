About Development

I started developing android apps in the year 2016. Interest came from my love towards the open-source platform Android and also because of the familiarity I had with Java, XML. I Use Android Studio IDE for my development. All the icon related resources are built using Adobe Photoshop 7.0.

I have released 2 apps in the Market which is available in Google play store for free and 1 pro feature packed app for a legitimate prize. Many More apps will be coming soon!

List of Apps Published

1. HandCricket Reborn

This game is an effort to help people recall their childhood version of playing Cricket with just a hand. For those who don't know, This is a game which can be played with hands, in which fingers are used to draw a score. If both players draw the same number, batsman is declared to be OUT. Otherwise, the batsman's score is added to count. It's interesting. Have a look for more information.

2. Attendance Register

This is an Android app for teaching staffs to recorn and maintain students' attendances digitally in their smartphone. They can categorize classes, take attendance, see reports, save and share reports, edit and update attendances. One important feature is "skipping of roll numbers" which is first in the play store. This app comes in 2 variant, free and pro. There are lot of more features in the pro version. Have a look!
